Classic Greece



Tour details



Tour length : 7D/6N Starts at ₹1,50,980Tourid: greece


21 - 28 June 22


Day 1: Arrive Athens


Upon arrival at Athens International Airport and transfer to the hotel.Rest of the day is to spend to explore this fascinating city Athens. The city with the most glorious history in the world is worshipped by gods and people. The most important civilization of the ancient world flourished in Athens and relives through superb architectural masterpieces. Overnight at the hotel in Athens.


Day 2: Athens City tour with Acropolis museum


Today after breakfast from the Acropolis to Omonia Square, with the half-day Athens sightseeing and Acropolis museum tour you will see all the famous sites of ancient and modern Athens on this comprehensive morning tour. Offering a perfect overview of the city, this guided half-day tour we start with Panathinaikon Stadium where the first modern Olympic Games took place in 1896. It is the only stadium in the world that is built out of white marble. Then proceed, passing by Zappeion and The Temple of Olympian Zeus, Athens Trilogy which includes the University of Athens, National Library, Hadrian’s Arch, St. Paul’s Church, The Parliament, The Tomb of the Unknown soldier, Schliemann’s House, Catholic Cathedral, Old Parliament, Constitution Square, Russian Orthodox Church, and finally the ACROPOLIS where we will visit Propylaea, Temple of Athena Nike, Parthenon, and the Erechtheion with its Porch of Maidens. Overnight at the hotel in Athens. (B)


Day 3: Athens Mykonos


Today after breakfast, you will check out from hotel in Athens and transferred to Piraeus port for ferry embarkation to Mykonos Island. Mykonos, famously called 'the island of the winds' due to the constant wind that blows, is the most popular and cosmopolitan island of the Cyclades and considered as one the most famous islands of the world. Arrival in Mykonos and transferred at the hotel. Rest of the day is free in order to explore the island. Overnight at the hotel in Mykonos. (B)


Day 4: Mykonos


Today the day is at leisure in Mykonos. The island is a paradise for water sport enthusiasts! It is only natural that the “Island of the Winds” should attract surfers and sailors from all over the world! There is a great choice of beaches for windsurfing. Overnight at the hotel in Mykonos. (B)

Day 5: Mykonos Santorini


Today after breakfast transfer to the port for the ferry to Santorini. Arrival in Santorini and transfer to the hotel in Santorini. Check into the hotel. Overnight at the hotel in Santorini. (B)


Day 6: Santorini-Volcano Tour


Today, after breakfast you will be picked up for a cruise at the caldera and its hot springs. During this tour, you will have the unique chance to visit the active volcano of Santorini, walk on dried lava up to its peak, feel the heat underneath your feet and see the steam of sulphuric gases emerging from the earth bowels. Next, you will sail to the thermal springs where you can dive or step down into the blue waters in order to swim towards the springs. Last you will visit the traditional sister island of Santorini, Thirassia, where you can either swim, go up to the village or simply relax and have something to eat. Return at your hotel. Overnight at the hotel in Santorini. (B)


Day 7: Depart Santorini


Today, after breakfast you will be transferred at Santorini airport for your departure flight via Athens to board flight back home or to your next destination. (B)
